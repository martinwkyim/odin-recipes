Tofu Pudding

Description

Tofu pudding is a traditional dessert made from filtered soy milk and coagulated with calcium sulphate. The texture is more velvety than ordinary tofu. At Station Tofu Pudding located in Tai O, you can enjoy the sweet and silky tofu pudding and other hot picks such as black sesame paste and walnut paste.
